Our story

Started by frustration. Built on results.

AstroCloud Digital began with a simple, frustrating observation: across Nairobi, hard-working business owners were losing customers every day — not because their service was poor, but because their online presence didn't reflect how good they really are.

Some had no website at all. Others had one built years ago that loaded slowly, broke on phones, and gave visitors no reason to get in touch. Meanwhile, their competitors with cleaner, faster, search-friendly sites were quietly winning the business.

So we set out to build websites the way they should be: fast, mobile-first, easy to find on Google, and engineered to turn visitors into real enquiries. Not digital brochures — working tools that grow your revenue.

Today we partner with SMEs across Kenya — from law firms and clinics to tour operators, schools and tradespeople — and we treat every project like our own business depends on it. Because, in a way, it does.
