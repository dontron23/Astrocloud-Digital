We process your data on the basis of your consent and/or to take steps at your request before entering into a contract. You may withdraw consent at any time.

We do not sell your data. We may share it with trusted service providers (for example email or form-processing tools) strictly to deliver our services, and only where they offer appropriate protection.

We keep your data only as long as necessary for the purposes above or as required by law, after which it is securely deleted.

Under the Data Protection Act, 2019 you have the right to access, correct, delete or object to the processing of your personal data, and to lodge a complaint with the ODPC. To exercise these rights, email us at hello@astrocloud.digital.

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data. No method of transmission over the internet is fully secure, however, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
