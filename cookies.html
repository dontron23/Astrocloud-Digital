1. What cookies are

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help websites work and understand how visitors use them.

2. How we use them

  • Essential: needed for the site to function (for example, remembering your cookie choice).
  • Analytics: privacy-friendly, aggregated statistics so we can improve the site. These are only set if you accept.

3. Managing cookies

You can accept or decline non-essential cookies using the banner shown on your first visit, and you can change your browser settings to block or delete cookies at any time. Blocking essential cookies may affect how the site works.

4. Third parties

Some cookies may be set by third-party services we use (such as analytics or embedded booking tools). Please refer to their own policies for details.
